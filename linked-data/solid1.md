# Solid

I had heard about [Solid](https://archive.fosdem.org/2023/schedule/event/sovcloud_from_zero_to_hero_with_solid/) from ⚔️ sir [TBL 🧎](https://www.w3.org/People/Berners-Lee/) a few years ago already, but only today I've actually tried it out; like this:

1. Create your Pod, e.g. at [Inrupt](https://start.inrupt.com/profile) (or another [hosted pod service](https://solidproject.org/for-developers#hosted-pod-services), not tested)

1. Use an App, such as [Media Kraken](https://noeldemartin.github.io/media-kraken/), chose to _store your data_ using Solid, Login with your own https://id.inrupt.com/YOU, add your favorite movies!

1. Now, to look "behind the curtain" and "under the hood", look at your personal RDF content, using a UI or as [raw 🐢 Turtle](https://docs.enola.dev/concepts/turtle/), using a _Pod Browser_ such as [Penny](https://penny.vincenttunru.com/) - no [Take Out](https://takeout.google.com) of your 🔗 Linked Data needed!

This is actually pretty cool...

PS: Maybe (my) [Enola.dev](https://docs.enola.dev) will support Solid, one day.
